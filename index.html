My 3-Day Split

Workout days: Monday, Wednesday, Thursday. Rest the other days.
Working sets: usually 1 hard set per exercise (warm up as needed).
Mon: Workout A Wed: Workout B Thu: Workout C Rest: Tue/Fri/Sat/Sun
Quick Notes
• “Positive failure” = you cannot complete another full lifting rep with good form.
• Warm-ups: do 1–2 lighter warm-up sets for big lifts (squat, deadlift, press, row).
• Rest: usually 60–120 seconds; big compounds can be 2–3 minutes if needed.
